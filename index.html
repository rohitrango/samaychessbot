Cyborg Vidit (2700)

GM Vidit Gujrathi is an Indian Chess Grandmaster. As of January 2021, he is the third highest rated player in India, with a rating of 2726. He was the Captain of Indian team in FIDE Online Chess Olympiad 2020, where India and Russia were declared joint winners. He also won the Biel Chess Festival Grandmaster tournament in 2019. Apart from winning tons of medals for India, he also streams chess, and also dabbling into stand-up comedy as well.
